About me

I am a very artistic and aspirant person and I have always had big goals for myself; I believe in never settling for less. Ever since I was a child, I always believed in making a differnece and bringing about positive change in this world. As I grew older, I learned that technology is the medium of rapid change and empowerment. I get my artistic side of things from my love for cars; I love to draw, sketch, model, and paint cars.

Our comapny went global and the different experiences from different countries had me brainstroming different ideas of what I can do in the field of IT. I then went ahead and did my undergrad in Comp. Sci. and since we dealt with a majority of clients that have web based requirements, I decided to start my journey from the web.
